# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

- High-end buyers seeking bespoke, handmade luxury footwear.
- Style-conscious individuals looking for custom sizing and craft.
- Private clients commissioning bespoke footwear.

## Product Purpose

To showcase the craftsmanship, browse collections, and place custom orders for Nelson shoes.

## Positioning

Crafted without compromise. Handmade luxury footwear built for excellence.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary and secondary CTA: Collections and Order Now.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: Crafted without compromise. Handmade luxury footwear built for excellence.
- Belief ladder:
  1. This is a premium luxury footwear brand.
  2. The shoes are entirely handmade with absolute precision and custom sizing.
  3. Ordering bespoke footwear is a seamless, exclusive experience.
- Proof on hand: High-end bespoke photography of shoes and customization stages.

## Brand Personality

Bespoke, Uncompromising, Excellent, Heritage, Craft, Premium, Bold, Sophisticated, Modern.

## Anti-references

- SaaS tech layouts
- Startup/tech templates
- Neon/glow or purple gradients
- Generic SaaS-cream templates

## Design Principles

- **Uncompromising Precision:** Every seam and detail must reflect absolute craftsmanship.
- **Legacy Showcase:** Let the photography of the physical shoes and processes carry the weight of the design.
- **Exclusive Restraint:** Simple, premium color strategy and layouts without startup gimmicks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Responsive layout across all viewports.
- Clear contrast ratios for text on dark and light surfaces.
- Minimal motion by default, respecting prefers-reduced-motion settings.
